Tutorial on how to use the flashcards

Welcome to the Flashcards tutorial! This guide will help you understand how to use the flashcards effectively.

How to Use the Flashcards

Click on the "Next" button to view the next flashcard.
Click on the "Previous" button to go back to the previous flashcard.
To flip a card and see the answer, click on the card itself.
The counter at the top will show you how many cards you have viewed.
